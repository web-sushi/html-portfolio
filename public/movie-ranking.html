The Best Movies According to Atsushi

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time

Good Will Hunting

This is a 1997 American Drama Film. It's directed by Gus Van Sant and written by Ben Affleck and Matt Damon. It stars Robin Williams, Ben Affleck, and Matt Damon. I like this movie a lot because of its impact in my life. It served as an inspiration for me to be determined to succeed despite the challenges in life.

Up

This is an animated movie produced by Pixar Animation Studios for Walt Disney Pictures. I like this movie a lot because of the combination of two genres, comedy and drama. It made me laugh and cry at the same time. It`s one of the movies that'll make you feel the importance of family.

The Karate Kid 2010 Film

This is a martial arts drama film directed by Harald Zwart from a screenplay by Christopher Murphey. The lead actors in this movie were Jaden Smith and Jackie Chan. I like this movie a lot because when I was a kid, I often watch a lot of martial arts movie and Jackie Chan is one of my favorite actor in this Genre. I think I've watched this movie more than 10 times already.
